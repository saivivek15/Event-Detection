117 words 27 July 2013 11:30 All Africa AFNWS English Jul 27, 2013 (Aswat Masriya/All Africa Global Media via COMTEX) -- At least 20 people were killed and 177 were injured in clashes that erupted early Saturday between supporter of deposed Islamist President Mohamed Mursi and the security forces, according to the health ministry as reported by the state news agency
The violence erupted in the early hours of Saturday between the security forces and Mursi loyalists in Cairo's Nasr City district near their sit-in at the Rabaa al-Adawia Mosque
The makeshift field hospital of the sit-in had reported that at least 75 were killed and nearly thousand were injured in the clashes
