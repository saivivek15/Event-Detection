107 words 16 August 2013 07:30 All Africa AFNWS English Aug 16, 2013 (Aswat Masriya/All Africa Global Media via COMTEX) -- Egypt's Health Ministry said that 578 were killed and 4,201 were injured in Wednesday's clashes in several Egyptian cities
In a statement issued on Thursday, the ministry said that 228 were killed and 1,056 were injured in Cairo's Rabaa al-Adaweya Square alone
In Nahda Square, the clashes toll rose to 90 dead and 613 injured, the statement added
Security forces launched a crackdown early Wednesday morning on the Muslim Brotherhood's sit-ins at Rabaa al-Adaweya and Nahda Squares
