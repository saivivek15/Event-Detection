115 words 17 August 2013 15:01 All Africa AFNWS English Aug 17, 2013 (Aswat Masriya/All Africa Global Media via COMTEX) -- Alexandria's ambulance authority head, Amr Nasr, said that at least 21 were killed in Alexandria on Friday
Nasr added that 101 were injured in clashes, 23 of whom were shot dead
Ambulances transported the 21 bodies to Kom al-Dekka Morgue, Nasr said
There is another unspecified number of bodies that have not been taken to the morgue yet, he added
He explained that ambulances transported 44 wounded people to hospitals, including the 23 who were shot
Security forces in Alexandria ordered the arrest of 130 demonstrators who were involved in Friday's clashes
