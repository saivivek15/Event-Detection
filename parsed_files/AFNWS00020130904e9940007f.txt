by Lydia Matata    78 words 4 September 2013 03:22 All Africa AFNWS English Sep 04, 2013 (The Star/All Africa Global Media via COMTEX) -- Tension is high in Trans Mara West where eight people have been confirmed dead following conflict between members of the Maasai and Kisii community
Approximately 150 families have also been displaced in the conflict
The families have taken refuge in two temporary camps, Lokoria and Ongeserian in Transmara West
The Kenya Red Cross is currently assessing the situation
